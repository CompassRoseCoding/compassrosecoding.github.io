Custom Websites for Unique Organizations

Creating a website for your small business, club, or organization can be a daunting task. Each organization has different needs, and not everyone can simply use a filled-in template to represent their organization to the world. Compass Rose Coding provides fully custom websites and hosting assistance and turns making your own website into an adventure.


What Does Your Organization Need?

Does your business need an engaging spash page, or an informational website detailing your products and services? Perhaps your organization needs a fun, engaging website to educate the public about your cause. Maybe you want a place for your club to communicate about sponsored events, or your church needs to let your congregation know about the need for volunteers in your ministry. Compass Rose Coding can help with all those cases!


Designing A Website

Website design can be daunting. Do you want a clean, modern site, with only the information you need and your logo? Would you like a punchy, dynamic site to catch your customer's attention? What colors or fonts would you like to use, or would you like to aim for a specific style? The Design page has a list of questions you can ask yourself and several tools to help you pick styles and color schemes.
